Privacy Policy

This app runs entirely in your browser. Your sleep data is never sent to any server controlled by the app developer. When you sign in with Google, the data goes directly from Google to your browser. Nothing is stored anywhere except on your own device.

How Your Data is Protected

  • Static client-side SPA with no backend server, database, or analytics.
  • OAuth 2.0 PKCE flow — access and refresh tokens stored in localStorage, cleared when you sign out.
  • All API requests go directly from your browser to health.googleapis.com
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts.
  • Revoke access anytime in your Google Account permissions.
